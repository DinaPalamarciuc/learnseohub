📥 How to export from Google Search Console

1
Open Google Search Console → Performance → Search results
2
Click Date: Last 28 days → switch to Compare tab → select Last 28 days vs previous 28 days → Apply
3
Scroll to the Queries table → sort by Impressions: Difference (highest first)
4
Click Export → choose Excel (recommended) or CSV
5
Upload above. The tool filters for queries with impressions +50% and position below 10.

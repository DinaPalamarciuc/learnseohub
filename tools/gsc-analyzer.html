📥 How to export the right CSV from Google Search Console

1
Open Google Search Console → Performance → Search results
2
Click Date: Last 28 days → switch to Compare tab → select Last 28 days vs previous 28 days → Apply
3
Scroll to the Queries table → sort by Impressions: Difference (highest first)
4
Click Export (top right of the table) → choose CSV
5
Upload the file above. The tool keeps only queries with impressions +50% and position below 10 — your real opportunities.

📥 How to export from Google Search Console

1
Open Google Search Console → Performance → Search results
2
Click the date range → switch to Compare → select "Last 28 days vs previous 28 days" → Apply
3
Scroll to the Queries table → sort by Impressions: Difference (↓ descending)
4
Click Export ↓ → choose Excel (recommended) or CSV
5
Upload above. The tool keeps only queries with +50% impressions growth and position 11–100 — the sweet spot for content action.

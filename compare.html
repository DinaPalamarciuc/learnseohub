Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use GEO-SEO Architect instead of Ahrefs or Semrush?
They serve different purposes. Ahrefs and Semrush excel at keyword research, rank tracking, and backlink analysis. GEO-SEO Architect excels at content creation, GEO optimization, and AI-ready output. Many professionals use Ahrefs/Semrush for research and GEO-SEO Architect for execution.
Which tool is best for a beginner without a tech team?
GEO-SEO Architect is designed for this — it produces code-ready HTML with Schema markup built in, so you don't need a developer to implement SEO-optimized content. Surfer SEO is also beginner-friendly for content scoring.
Do I need to pay monthly for GEO-SEO Architect?
GEO-SEO Architect uses a license-based model rather than a traditional SaaS monthly subscription. Visit the pricing page for current plans — there's also a free 3-day demo to test all features.

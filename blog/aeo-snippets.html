Answer Engine Optimization (AEO): Win Featured Snippets

How to capture Position Zero, optimize for voice search, and dominate zero-click results in Google's evolving SERP.

What is Position Zero?

Featured Snippets appear above the #1 organic result. Three main types:

  • Paragraph: 40-60 words directly answering a question
  • List: Numbered or bulleted steps/items
  • Table: Structured comparison data

To win: provide a direct, concise answer immediately after your heading.

Voice & AI Assistants

Voice queries are longer and more conversational than typed searches. To optimize:

  • Use full question headings ("How do I...")
  • Write FAQ sections with natural language answers
  • Include NAP data for local queries
  • Structure content for quick, spoken delivery
Key: Voice search is growing 25% YoY. If your content can be spoken aloud and still make sense, you're optimized for voice.

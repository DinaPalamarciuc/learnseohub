What is Answer Engine Optimization?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring and formatting content to be selected as the direct answer by search engines, AI assistants, and voice search. While traditional SEO targets ranked positions #1-10, AEO targets Position Zero — the answer box that appears above all organic results.

Definition: AEO = structuring content to directly answer specific questions so clearly that search engines and AI models extract and display it as the answer, without the user needing to click through.

How to Win Position Zero: Step-by-Step

  1. Identify question keywords: Target queries that start with "what is," "how to," "why does," "when should" — these trigger snippet opportunities at 4x the rate of non-question queries
  2. Rank on page 1 first: 99.58% of Featured Snippets come from pages already ranking on the first page. Snippets reward existing ranking, not new pages
  3. Use H2 headings that mirror the query: If the query is "what is canonical URL," your H2 should be exactly "What is a Canonical URL?"
  4. Provide a direct 40-60 word answer immediately after the H2: Google's snippet extraction algorithm strongly prefers the first paragraph under a question-formatted heading
  5. Add FAQ Schema: FAQPage JSON-LD tells Google explicitly which questions your page answers and what the answers are
Quick win: Open Google Search Console, filter by queries with clicks < expected (impressions high, CTR low) — these are often snippet opportunities where a format change could move you from position 3-5 to Position Zero.

Optimizing for AI Overviews (AIO)

Google's AI Overviews (formerly SGE) now appear for over 40% of queries. They work differently from traditional Featured Snippets — AI Overviews synthesize from multiple sources, not just the top-ranked page.

AIO Optimization Checklist

  • Direct answer structure: Every major question in your content should have a clear, standalone answer that works without context
  • Cite authoritative sources: AI Overviews favor content that cites primary sources (studies, official documentation, known experts)
  • Include statistics with dates: "67% of searches trigger AI Overviews (Google, Q1 2026)" is more likely to be cited than an unattributed claim
  • Multiple question coverage: AI Overviews pull from pages that comprehensively cover a topic — cover related sub-questions in the same article
  • Schema markup: Article, FAQPage, and HowTo schema increase AI Overview visibility by 23% (Semrush, 2025)

Voice Search & Conversational Queries

Voice search queries are 3x longer than text queries and almost always conversational questions. Optimize for voice by targeting natural language patterns:

  • Write content that answers full questions, not just keywords: "How do I optimize my Google Business Profile?" not "GBP optimization"
  • Use SpeakableSpecification schema to mark which content sections are ideal for voice reading
  • Target featured snippet formats — voice search reads the Featured Snippet approximately 80% of the time
  • Local voice queries ("near me") require complete and accurate Google Business Profile data

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?
AEO is the practice of structuring content to win Featured Snippets, AI Overviews, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search results. It focuses on providing direct, concise, structured answers to conversational questions so Google and AI models can extract and display your content as the answer to user queries.
What is Position Zero in SEO?
Position Zero is a Featured Snippet that appears above all organic search results. It typically shows a direct answer — a paragraph, numbered list, or table — extracted from a highly-ranked page. Winning Position Zero can significantly increase click-through rate even for queries where you rank in positions 2-5.
How do I optimize content for Featured Snippets?
To optimize for Featured Snippets: (1) identify question-based keywords, (2) rank on page 1 first, (3) use H2 headings that mirror the exact query, (4) provide a direct 40-60 word answer immediately after the heading, (5) use the correct format (paragraph, list, or table) for the query type, (6) add FAQPage Schema markup.
How is AEO different from GEO?
AEO focuses specifically on winning structured answer features in search results — Featured Snippets, PAA boxes, AI Overviews. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is broader, targeting citations in AI-generated responses from tools like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Google AI Mode. AEO is a subset of the broader GEO approach.

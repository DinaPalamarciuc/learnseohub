Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026 · Applies to learnseohub.com

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit — such as your language preference or whether you have accepted our cookie notice.

2. Cookies We Use

3. Cookie Categories

Essential Cookies (always active)

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They cannot be disabled. They do not track you for advertising purposes and do not collect personal information beyond what is necessary for the site to work.

  • Remembering your cookie consent choice
  • Remembering your display preferences (dark/light mode)

Analytics Cookies (optional — requires your consent)

We do not currently use analytics cookies. If we add them in the future (e.g., Google Analytics), they will only be activated after you explicitly consent via our cookie banner, and this policy will be updated accordingly.

Marketing Cookies (optional — requires your consent)

We do not currently use marketing or advertising cookies. We do not run retargeting campaigns or use tracking pixels. If this changes, we will update this policy and request your consent before activating any such cookies.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We do not intentionally load third-party cookies. However, if you click on an affiliate link (e.g., to GEO-SEO Architect at seo-geo-arhitect.top), the destination website may set its own cookies. We have no control over those cookies and they are governed by the third party's cookie policy.

5. How to Manage Cookies

Using our Cookie Settings button

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the 🍪 button visible in the bottom-left corner of any page. This will re-display the consent banner and allow you to update your choices.

Using your browser settings

You can also control cookies directly through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note: disabling essential cookies may cause parts of the site to not function correctly (e.g., your dark mode preference will not be saved).

6. localStorage

In addition to cookies, we use browser localStorage — a similar technology — to store your theme preference (dark/light mode) and consent choice. localStorage data is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear it via your browser's developer tools (Application → Local Storage → learnseohub.com → Delete).

If we add new cookies or change how we use existing ones, we will update this policy and, where required by law, request your consent again via the cookie banner.

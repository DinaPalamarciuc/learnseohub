Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about modern Search Engine Optimization.

What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?
GEO is the process of optimizing content to be easily understood, verified, and cited by AI language models and generative search engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. It involves clear entity relationships and direct answers.
How is AEO different from traditional SEO?
While traditional SEO focuses on ranking ten blue links for keyword queries, AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) focuses on providing direct, concise, and structured answers to conversational questions to win Featured Snippets and AI Overviews.
Are the tools on LearnSEO Hub free to use?
Yes, all interactive tools, including the SERP Previewer, Schema Generator, and Crawl Checker, are 100% free to use directly in your browser without creating an account or providing an email address.

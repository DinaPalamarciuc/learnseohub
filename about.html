Why this exists

SEO is changing faster than most learning resources can keep up with. AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews are reshaping how content gets discovered — and most guides are still written for 2019.

LearnSEO Hub was built to close that gap: practical, up-to-date resources that reflect how search actually works today.
